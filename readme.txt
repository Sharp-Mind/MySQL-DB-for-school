/ * The project involves the use of a database in the context of a modern educational institution, in this case, a school, and a website for it.
 * Since the integrity and reliability of information is better achieved if it is stored in one place, then for the implementation of school
 * journal and diary web environment is great. Students will never forget what was asked in the lesson or what physics lesson tomorrow;
 * teachers will visually calculate statistics and quickly fill in their homework; parents will know about the progress of their children and will be aware of school
 * events and activities.
 *
 * In my project, such features as accounting for the presence of employees and students using cards are not implemented (in modern schools such functionality
 * present), operations with a balance on it, features of examinations and the beginning / end of the year, since I paid attention to the main functions,
 * which, in my opinion, should be on the school portal, as well as statistics.
